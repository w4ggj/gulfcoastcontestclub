Who we are
About the club

NE2CC is based in Reddington Beach, FL — one of the best DX locations in the southeastern United States.

Serious contesting

We compete in all major HF contests year-round and submit scores under the NE2CC club banner.

Gulf Coast advantage

Reddington Beach offers excellent propagation paths to Europe, the Caribbean, and South America.

Open to all hams

General class license required. We welcome new contesters and provide elmering for those getting started.
