Submit your score

After each contest, submit your claimed score to 3830scores.com and list Gulf Coast Contest Club (NE2CC) as your club. This feeds into our club aggregate standings.

Official results

Adjudicated results are posted by the contest sponsor (ARRL, CQ) several months after the event. Scores on this page are claimed scores and may differ slightly from final adjudicated results.

Missing your score?

If you're an NE2CC member and your score isn't listed here, contact us with the contest name, your callsign, and claimed score and we'll add it.
