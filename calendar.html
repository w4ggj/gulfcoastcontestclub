Score submission

Submit all claimed scores to 3830scores.com and list NE2CC as your club affiliation. This counts toward club aggregate standings in ARRL and CQ contests.

Logging software

Club members use N1MM+, Win-Test, and N3FJP depending on the contest. Reach out via the contact form if you need help getting set up with contest logging.

Field Day

ARRL Field Day is our biggest club event of the year. We operate as a group from a local site — operators, loggers, and helpers all welcome. Watch News for site announcements.
