News & updates

Contest recaps, Field Day announcements, member news, and club updates.

April 2025
Field Day 2025 — site confirmed, operators wanted

GCCC will operate from a waterfront location in the Reddington Beach area for ARRL Field Day 2025. We're planning a 2A operation and welcome all licensed club members to participate as operators, loggers, or support crew.

Field Day is one of the most public-facing events in amateur radio — a great chance to operate with the club and introduce the hobby to the community. More details on frequency assignments, operating shifts, and equipment will be shared via club net in the coming weeks.

If you're interested in helping out or operating, reach out through the contact form. All General class and above are welcome.

December 2024
CQ WW CW 2024 — GCCC posts competitive score

Members operating under the GCCC banner turned in a strong performance in the 2024 CQ WW CW contest. Conditions on 20 and 15 meters were favorable, with solid European runs on Saturday and good Caribbean activity throughout the weekend.

The club multi-single operation finished with a claimed score of 2,847,320 points — a solid result for the West Central Florida section. Individual operator scores are posted on 3830scores.com under the GCCC club affiliation.

Thanks to everyone who got on the air and submitted under the club banner. Full results will be updated on the results page once adjudicated scores are published by CQ.

October 2024
New members welcome — enrollment open

GCCC is open to new members. If you hold a General class license or above and have an interest in HF contesting — whether you're experienced or just getting started — we'd love to hear from you.

We can help you get set up on major contest logging programs like N1MM+, talk through antenna strategies for the Florida coastal environment, and get you connected with other active operators in the club. Contesting is more fun with a group, and submitting your scores under the NE2CC affiliation contributes to club standings in CQ and ARRL events.

Use the contact form to reach out. No dues, no complicated process — just a love of HF and a desire to compete.

July 2024
ARRL Field Day 2024 recap

The club completed another successful ARRL Field Day operation in June 2024. Operating in the 2A category, GCCC accumulated 4,210 points over the 27-hour event.

Conditions were mixed — 20 meters was reliable throughout Saturday afternoon and evening, while 40 meters opened up nicely after local sunset. 10 meters offered some surprise openings on Sunday morning that yielded a solid run of contacts before the band faded.

Thanks to all operators, loggers, and support crew who participated. Field Day is always one of the best days of the year to be a ham radio operator. See you next June.

December 2023
CQ WW CW 2023 — results posted

Results are in for the 2023 CQ WW CW contest. The GCCC multi-single operation finished with a claimed score of 2,214,860 — a solid performance from the West Central Florida section.

Conditions were particularly strong on 15 meters this cycle, with long-path openings to Asia and consistent European runs through Saturday evening. The club will be looking to improve on this in the 2024 season.

All scores are posted on the results page and on 3830scores.com.
